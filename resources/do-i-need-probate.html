Do I Need Probate in Nevada?

Probate may depend on ownership, value, beneficiary designations, and trust funding.

Start with the structure

Probate is not determined by the existence of a will alone. A review typically begins with how each asset is titled, whether a beneficiary is named, whether the asset is held in trust, and what transfer procedure may be available.

Practical next step

Gather account statements, deeds, vehicle titles, beneficiary information, the will, trust documents, and an approximate asset-and-debt list.
